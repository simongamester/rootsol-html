How we work

  • After Care

    We offer a range of support options after completing a project. This can range from routine maintenance to feature updates and functional refinements.

  • Compatibility

    All of our websites are designed to be compatible with recent versions of Internet Explorer, Google Chrome, Firefox and Safari.

  • 30 Day Warranty

    We operate a stringent testing process and we offer a warranty of 30 days after our sites go live to make amends free of charge.

  • Accessibility

    Our websites can be made to comply with Disability Discrimination Act (DDA) regulations and Website Accessibility Initiative (WAI) guidelines when required.
